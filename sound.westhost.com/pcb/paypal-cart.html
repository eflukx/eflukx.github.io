For the convenience of overseas buyers, I have added a PayPal shopping cart. All prices are in USD, but if you are in Australia or wish to pay using AUD, the traditional methods for purchase are all still available. These include 'traditional' PayPal purchases, or credit cards using fax, password protected PDF or the ESP secure order form. To see the full range of options available, please see the ESP Purchase Information Page.

If you need more than one PCB of a particular type, simply change the quantity in the PayPal cart and click on 'update'. Project information is available by clicking on the project number - for example P05 (left side of the screen). Javascript must be enabled in your browser or this will not work. My apologies for the extremely annoying flashing note below (if your browser allows it) - I don't know how, but people keep missing it!

Please ensure that you include P+P (pack and post - first item in the list) - orders cannot be completed if postage is not paid. In addition, I end up paying more in PayPal fees and it's very inconvenient to have to send emails to request that pack+post be paid before I can process the order.

If you have any issues with the shopping cart, please let me know. It's new, and there may be bugs that I haven't found during testing.
