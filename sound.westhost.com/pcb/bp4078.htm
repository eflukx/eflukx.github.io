Elliott Sound Products BP4078 - 400W Class-D Power Module 

No Longer Available - Please contact ColdAmp directly.

This is a complete Class-D amplifier module, requiring only a power supply. Featuring on-board low voltage regulators, over and under voltage detection, clipping detector and thermal sensing, there are many options (see the Datasheet for full details). The module comprises a complete 400W (4 Ohms) amplifier, with balanced input capability.
