Elliott Sound Products Project 113 - Stereo Headphone Amplifier 

The PCB comprises a complete stereo hi-fi headphone amplifier. You need only to add a small heatsink and power supply (P05 Rev A is recommended).

Performance of the headphone amp is exemplary, and it is easily the equal of a great many very expensive commercial high end designs.

The P113 headphone amp can also be used as a spring reverb amplifier, both for drive and recovery. Complete details available in the secure site.
