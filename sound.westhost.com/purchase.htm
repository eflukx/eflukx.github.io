 International Customers

Use the GST free board prices listed - your credit card will be billed in Australian Dollars, and the final price that appears on your card statement is determined by the exchange rate prevailing when the card details are processed. Click on the button in the header for more information, currency conversion details, etc.   All orders are sent using Australia Post services, insurance is optional, though not usually needed. For those countries where insurance is really needed, it is not available. (I didn't make the rules, so please don't ask.)

If you wish to pay using USD (American Dollar) funds, you must use PayPal. I cannot bill credit cards in any any currency other than AUD.

Please Note ... For overseas customers, cheques (or 'checks') are not acceptable. Any cheque drawn on a foreign bank requires a special trip to the bank, incurs (often very heavy) fees, and makes the process completely uneconomical. No other online merchant accepts foreign cheques, and ESP is no different. I realise that this may be inconvenient, but it is also necessary because of the huge impost on my time (plus, I receive far less than the order is worth).

Australian Customers

For local customers, please note that the GST included price must be used for all orders. A personal or bank cheque is acceptable - but goods will not be shipped until the cheque is cleared. The preferred payment methods are Visa, Mastercard, Australian Post Office money order or Paymate.

Australian residents using PayPal must use the AUD price (including GST), and payment must be in Australian dollars. Goods are sent using Australia Post.

Shipping And Documentation

Boards are normally shipped in a padded postal bag via normal airmail, and this is at the rate shown in the price list. Where possible, orders are sent as 'large letter' in a padded bag. Customs forms are not required by Australia Post for the padded bags, and are not used unless the package has to be sent as a 'parcel' (anything that is bigger than a large letter). Click on the button in the header for more information.

Where registered mail (Australia), courier or insurance (RoW - Rest of World) is requested, the fee shown in the pricelist is in addition to pack and postage charges.

In common with the postal services in most countries, normal airmail post does not include any form of tracking. If you want the ability to track your order, you will have to use the courier option. Please do not ask for tracking numbers if your package is delayed, because there isn't one unless you pay for it. If you do pay for international courier, I will send you the tracking number.

Please Note: Orders less than AU$50.00 (excluding pack and post) may not be insured except by prior arrangement.

Please see the pricelist for more information about pack and post options.

Order Codes
The order code for each PCB or other product is shown in the price list. Where there is more than one board for a project, they will have the suffix A, B, (etc.) - for example P27a and P27b (guitar amplifier boards). All boards are made with fibreglass PCB material.  Some boards are sold in pairs, please note that paired boards will not be sold individually, nor in odd numbers.
Miscellaneous Info.
All board sizes are in millimetres and are approximate to the nearest millimetre. Board thickness is approx. 1.6mm unless otherwise stated.

Some boards have relatively small tracks and closely spaced components. These require a fine tipped soldering bit (preferably temperature controlled), and careful soldering techniques. If you are new to soldering components into a PCB, please exercise caution during construction, as it is possible to damage the board with excessive or prolonged heat.

Warranty

All goods are checked before shipping, but it is possible that a defective board or disk may be supplied. The boards are warranted to be free from defects that would make them unusable for the intended use. Such boards will be replaced free of charge or a refund given upon receipt of the defective board(s). Click on the button in the header for more information.

Order Forms

Load the HTML form, save a copy of the text form, or download the order encryption program. Please fill in all sections. Print a copy of order form to mail or fax, or e-mail the encrypted order.

Printed order forms (only) can be sent by FAX to a secure machine.  Please do not send cover sheets or other un-necessary sheets - all needed information is on the order form.  Printouts intended for fax must be printed (or neatly hand printed) in black, because fax machines do not handle blue very well. Notes:

  • Fax is intended for orders only - it is not for general enquiries, requests, etc. In most cases, requests for assistance or information sent via fax will be ignored. All that is achieved is that I become annoyed (fax machines are expensive to feed!), and you don't get the information you want. All normal communication must be sent via e-mail, otherwise you may never get an answer.

  • US Customers please note that +612 is the country and area code, and is not a US domestic area code.

  • When you send your order via fax, ! Please ! send me an e-mail with your address and confirmation of the order details (but without the card details of course). It is not uncommon that faxes arrive that are very hard to read - several have been completely illegible. If this happens, I have no way to contact you because the e-mail address and other details cannot be read.

By including your address, I will cut and paste it into the order. This is a lot faster than trying to read a blurry fax, and eliminates errors. I will also send confirmation by return e-mail. Because e-mail addresses are often very difficult to read on the faxed form, I don't even try to decipher them unless there is a problem with the order. Even that is regularly impossible if the fax quality is bad enough !
